Introduction

Academic stress is one of the most common challenges students face today. It can be caused by exams, deadlines, heavy workloads, and high expectations. While some stress can motivate students to perform better, too much stress can negatively affect their mental, emotional, and physical health.

Effects of Academic Stress

Mental Effects

Academic stress may lead to anxiety, difficulty concentrating, overthinking, and reduced motivation.

Emotional Effects

Students may experience mood swings, irritability, frustration, and feelings of being overwhelmed.

Physical Effects

Too much stress can cause headaches, sleep problems, fatigue, and weakened immune system.
